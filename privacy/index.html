Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Meme A Day Studio is a meme creation app for iPhone. This policy explains what data the app collects, how it is used, and how to contact us.

Data We Collect

We collect the following data to operate and improve the app:

  • An internal user ID created by our app infrastructure.
  • Product interaction analytics, such as app launches, taps, and feature usage.
  • Basic app and device context for analytics, such as app version, build number, OS version, and device model.
  • Saved meme creations, including exported photo, GIF, or video meme files, source media type, and caption text, when cloud backup is available.

How We Use Data

  • To provide app functionality, including saving and backing up your meme creations.
  • To understand how people use the app and improve reliability, performance, and product decisions.

Data We Do Not Collect for Tracking

We do not sell your data, use your data for third-party advertising, or track you across apps and websites owned by other companies. We do not collect contacts, precise location, payment information, or advertising identifiers.

Sharing and Exporting

When you choose to save a meme to Photos or share it through Messages, AirDrop, Mail, or another destination, that action is handled by iOS and the destination you select.

Service Providers

We use Supabase to provide app infrastructure, including anonymous authentication, cloud backup, and first-party analytics.

Data Requests

For privacy questions or deletion requests, email lambones@gmail.com.
